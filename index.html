TETRAOS
Personal ProjectWinter 2014

Worked on this game over winter break 2014, made with JavaScript and HTML5. This was supposed to be a smaller project that I could reasonably complete, so that I could show a finished product using my skills. It was helpful in teaching me to pay attention to details such as how blocks should interact once a smaller ring is cleared and a larger one must take its place. I also got to hear some feedback from the friends who tried the game, and I learned of a better scoring system and that my game over state was not communicated clearly enough. It was also my first time working with the canvas methods, so I just stuck to learning to draw squares, and I tried to emulate a Tetris style of animation on the blocks falling. However, I feel that the way the game handles blocks missing the center could have been designed better or if there was no better solution than sliding them, I should have made that more clear.
